<h2>Signs It's Time to Replace Your Power Tool</h2>

<div class="alert alert-warning">

Replace or service your tool if you notice any of the following:

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Motor overheating during normal use.</li>

<li>Burning smell while operating.</li>

<li>Excessive vibration.</li>

<li>Loss of power under light load.</li>

<li>Unusual noises from bearings or gears.</li>

<li>Damaged switches or loose electrical cords.</li>

<li>Visible sparks inside the motor housing.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<h2>Safety Tips While Using Power Tools</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow safe operating practices before using any power tool.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Read the manufacturer's operating instructions.</li>

<li>Wear safety glasses and hearing protection.</li>

<li>Use gloves suitable for the application.</li>

<li>Secure the workpiece before cutting or drilling.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Never operate damaged equipment.</li>

<li>Keep cables away from moving parts.</li>

<li>Use the correct accessory for each material.</li>

</ul>
