<h1>How to File Income Tax Return (ITR) Online in India – Complete Step-by-Step Guide (AY 2026-27)</h1>

<p>
Filing your Income Tax Return (ITR) online has become easier than ever through the Income Tax Department's e-filing portal. Whether you're a salaried employee, pensioner, freelancer, business owner, consultant, landlord, or investor, filing your ITR on time helps you stay compliant with tax laws, avoid penalties, and receive refunds faster.
</p>

<p>
Many taxpayers file their returns in just a few minutes, but thousands still make mistakes by selecting the wrong ITR form, forgetting to verify their return, ignoring AIS mismatches, or filing after the due date. These errors may result in notices, delayed refunds, or additional tax liability.
</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning">

<strong>Latest AY 2026-27 Update</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>The Income Tax e-Filing Portal has remained largely stable during this filing season.</li>

<li>Experts currently believe salaried taxpayers should not expect another extension of the filing deadline.</li>

<li>The government has already provided additional time for many eligible non-audit businesses and professionals, reducing filing pressure.</li>

<li>Waiting until the last day increases the chances of missing important details or facing heavy server traffic.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<h2>How to Verify Your Income Tax Return</h2>

<p>
Submitting your Income Tax Return is not the final step. Your return must also be verified within the prescribed time; otherwise, it may be treated as not filed. Verification confirms that the information submitted belongs to you and authorizes the Income Tax Department to process your return.
</p>
