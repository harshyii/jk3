<h2>Power Tool Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always inspect your machine before use and wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE).

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear ISI-approved safety goggles.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged operation.</li>

<li>Inspect power cords before every use.</li>

<li>Never operate damaged grinding discs.</li>

<li>Use the correct accessory for each machine.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Keep children away from the work area.</li>

<li>Read the manufacturer's instruction manual.</li>

</ul>

</div>
