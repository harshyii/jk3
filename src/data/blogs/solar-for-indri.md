<h2>Net Metering Explained</h2>

<p>

One of the biggest advantages of an on-grid solar system is <strong>Net Metering</strong>. During the daytime, your solar panels may produce more electricity than your building consumes. Instead of wasting this extra energy, it is exported to the electricity grid through a net meter.

</p>

<div class="alert alert-info">

<strong>Simple Example</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Your home uses 12 units during the day.</li>

<li>Your solar system generates 20 units.</li>

<li>The extra 8 units are sent to the electricity grid.</li>

<li>These exported units help reduce your future electricity bill according to applicable utility policies.</li>

</ul>

</div>
