<h2>Understanding Net Metering</h2>

<p>One of the biggest advantages of an <strong>On-Grid Solar System</strong> is the ability to use <strong>Net Metering</strong>. Instead of letting excess electricity go to waste, the surplus energy generated during the day is exported to the electricity grid. When your solar panels produce less electricity, such as at night or during cloudy weather, you can draw power back from the grid.</p>

<div class="alert alert-info">

<strong>How Net Metering Works</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>☀️ Solar panels generate electricity during the day.</li>
<li>🏠 Your home or business uses this electricity first.</li>
<li>⚡ Extra electricity is exported to the grid.</li>
<li>📊 The exported units are adjusted against the electricity you consume later.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<p>For most homes and commercial buildings in Pundri, net metering provides the fastest return on investment because it maximizes the value of every unit of electricity generated.</p>
