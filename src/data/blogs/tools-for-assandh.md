<h2>Essential Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow recommended safety practices when operating power tools.

<ul class="mt-3 mb-0">

<li>Wear safety goggles and gloves.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged work.</li>

<li>Disconnect the power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Inspect discs, blades and drill bits before use.</li>

<li>Never exceed the rated operating speed.</li>

<li>Keep the work area clean and well lit.</li>

<li>Avoid loose clothing near rotating tools.</li>

<li>Use the correct accessory for each application.</li>

</ul>
