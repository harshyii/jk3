<h2>Power Tool Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow these safety practices while operating power tools.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear safety glasses or a face shield.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged work.</li>

<li>Inspect accessories before use.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing blades or discs.</li>

<li>Use only accessories rated for the tool's speed.</li>

<li>Never remove safety guards.</li>

<li>Avoid loose clothing near rotating parts.</li>

<li>Maintain a stable working position.</li>

</ul>

</div>
