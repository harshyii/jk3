<h2>Power Tool Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow basic safety practices while operating power tools.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear safety glasses or a face shield.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection when required.</li>

<li>Inspect cables before every use.</li>

<li>Keep the work area clean and well lit.</li>

<li>Use the correct accessory for the machine.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing blades or discs.</li>

<li>Never remove factory-installed safety guards.</li>

<li>Wear appropriate gloves and safety shoes.</li>

</ul>

</div>
