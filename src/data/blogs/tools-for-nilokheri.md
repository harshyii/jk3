<h2>Essential Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

<strong>Always follow these safety practices while operating power tools:</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear approved safety goggles.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during extended work.</li>

<li>Wear suitable gloves whenever appropriate.</li>

<li>Inspect power cords before use.</li>

<li>Use the correct cutting or grinding accessory.</li>

<li>Never remove factory-installed safety guards.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Keep the work area clean and well illuminated.</li>

<li>Avoid loose clothing near rotating equipment.</li>

<li>Read the manufacturer's operating instructions.</li>

</ul>

</div>
