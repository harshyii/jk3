<h2>Safety Checklist</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow these basic safety practices before operating any power tool.

<ul class="mt-3 mb-0">

<li>Wear certified eye protection.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during extended work.</li>

<li>Inspect power cables before use.</li>

<li>Use the correct accessory for the machine.</li>

<li>Secure the workpiece before cutting or drilling.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Never remove factory-installed safety guards.</li>

<li>Keep children and bystanders away from the work area.</li>

</ul>
